Spandan - 2015

5th - 7th March 2015

Spandan @ IIITB – where adrenaline replaces serenity, where tech enthusiasts morph into passionate sportspersons and the only place where fifteen events are finished in three days. Spandan never ceases to bring out the youth from students and professors alike and gives everyone a well deserved break from the otherwise academically loaded schedule. In three days, it leaves the college gasping for breath and at the same time unites them through competition (ironic? - No! It's the true spirit of sports!) . The campus sees a vibrant mix of alumni during this time and doubles up as an informal platform to interact with them alongside engaging in sporting tussles. The spirit of sports triggered by Spandan lingers on for months and just when it seems to fade the next one is just around the corner! To cut a long story short – 'seeing is believing', so come be a part of Spandan to live in the moment and experience euphoria like never before!
